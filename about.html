About De La Salle John Bosco College

The De La Salle John Bosco College (DLSJBC) is a PAASCU-accredited Lasallian educational institution located in Mangagoy, Bislig City, Surigao del Sur.


Established in 1963 by the Don Bosco Fathers, the administration of the school was transferred to the De La Salle Brothers in 1977.


Today, DLSJBC offers Preschool, Elementary, Junior High School, Senior High School, College, Graduate School, and TESDA programs while continuing its mission of providing quality Christian education.

Our History

1963

The institution began as St. John Bosco Technical High School, founded by the Don Bosco Fathers to provide quality Catholic education.

1977

The De La Salle Brothers assumed the administration of the institution, continuing the Lasallian mission of excellence and service.

Today

DLSJBC continues to develop future professionals through academic excellence, Christian values, and community engagement.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide quality Catholic and Lasallian education that forms competent, compassionate, and committed individuals who serve God and society.

Vision

A leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, Christian formation, and responsible leadership.
