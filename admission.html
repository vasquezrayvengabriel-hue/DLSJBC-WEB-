Enrollment Procedures

(NEW) KINDERGARTEN

  1. See the Guidance – in – charge for the Entrance Examination / Interview.
  2. Secure the Eligibility Form from the Principal's Office.
  3. Present PSA Birth Certificate and Eligibility Form to the Registrar's Office. Secure and accomplish Registration Form.
  4. Proceed to Finance Office for Tuition Leveling.
  5. Pay the Enrollment Fee at the cashier.
  6. Go back to the Registrar's Office.

(NEW) GRADE 1 to GRADE 12

  1. Present the admission requirements at the Registrar's Office.
  2. Secure and accomplish the Registration Form.
  3. Proceed to the Finance Office for Tuition Leveling.
  4. Pay the Enrollment Fee at the cashier.
  5. Go back to the Registrar's Office.

CONTINUING STUDENTS/ PUPILS

  1. Present original report card to the Registrar's Office.
  2. Secure and accomplish the Registration Form.
  3. Proceed to the Finance Office for Tuition Leveling.
  4. Pay the Enrollment Fee at the cashier.
  5. Go back to the Registrar's Office.
