Admissions

Welcome to the Admissions Office of De La Salle John Bosco College. We are committed to making the enrollment process simple and convenient for all new and continuing students. Please review the admission requirements and enrollment procedures below.

View Requirements

Admission Requirements

Kindergarten

  • • PSA Birth Certificate
  • • Passport-size Photo
  • • Parent/Guardian ID

Elementary

  • • Report Card
  • • PSA Birth Certificate
  • • Good Moral Certificate

Junior & Senior High

  • • Form 138
  • • Good Moral Certificate
  • • PSA Birth Certificate
  • • Certificate of Completion

College Freshmen

  • • Report Card
  • • PSA Birth Certificate
  • • Good Moral Certificate
  • • Medical Certificate
  • • Drug Test Result
  • • 4 pcs 1x1 ID Picture

College Transferee

  • • Transcript of Records
  • • Honorable Dismissal
  • • Good Moral Certificate
  • • Medical Certificate
  • • Drug Test Result

TESDA / Diploma

  • • High School Report Card
  • • PSA Birth Certificate
  • • Barangay Certificate
  • • Residency Proof

Enrollment Procedure

Step 1

Submit all admission requirements to the Registrar's Office.

Step 2

Complete the Registration Form provided by the Registrar.

Step 3

Proceed to the Finance Office for tuition assessment.

Step 4

Pay the required enrollment fees.

Step 5

Return to the Registrar's Office to complete your enrollment.
